NYC Taxi Data Visualization



In this app, we will explore trip record data (courtesy of NYC OpenData) of NYC Yellow Taxis as well as High-Volume For-Hire Services(which currently consist of Uber, Lyft, Juno & Via). This data is represented on the map to the right via a heatmap of designated Taxi Zones in NYC. The lighter the tile, the more Pick Ups/Drop offs have taken place. Hover over a tile to see more detailed information.



Tips:
Buttons are labeled with type of data currently being displayed. Clicking the Buttons will toggle trip type(Pick Ups/Drop Offs) and vehicle type(Taxi/App Cars). The slider below the map allows you to see how the data changes from hour to hour. The final point on the slider displays aggregate data for the entire day.
